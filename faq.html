FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


What is an Awair?

An Awair is a sensor that determines the health of the room by measuring different air quality metrics


Why is this important?

It is important for people to know the health of the rooms they work in day in and day out


Who sponsors this project?

This project is sponsored by the Living Link Lab at the University of Virginia. For more information about the Living Link Lab click on the Living Link Lab section of the navbar
